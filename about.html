About ByteMe Decider


ByteMe Decider is a fun app that helps groups make decisions together!


How It Works:

1. Create a session for food, movies, or activities
2. Share your 6-digit code with friends
3. Everyone votes on their favorites
4. ByteMe finds the best match for your group!
