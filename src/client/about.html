About TieBreaker


TieBreaker was born from our frustration with endless group indecision. We've designed a platform that makes group choices easier, fairer, and completely unbiased.

By using an anonymous voting system we remove the peer pressure of social influence. This helps ensure results reflect what everyone actually wants.



How It Works

1. Create a session for food, movies, or activities.
2. Share your unique 6-digit code with friends.
3. Vote on your favorite choices anonymously.
4. Match! TieBreaker reveals the winner based on votes.
